AppHack X


Apphack X will begin on Friday, April 5th at 6pm and end on Saturday, April 6th at 12pm. For AppHack's tenth anniversary, we are expecting our largest turnout yet, with more sponsors and participants than ever before!


Be sure to register and sign up for the slack!

Rules


  1. No weapons or alcohol.
  2. Be respectful of other attendees.
  3. All coding and the majority of asset work must be done during the event (April 5th 6pm - April 6th 12pm).
    1. UMLs, concept art, and to-do lists are allowed beforehand.
  4. At least one team member must be present for the demo fair. Prizes will not be awarded to teams that are not present.
  5. All teams retain full ownership of what they create during AppHack.
  6. Category-specific rules:
    1. To win the Outstanding Freshman prize, everyone on the team must either be in CS 2440 (or equivalent) currently, OR have not yet taken it.
    2. To win the Women in CS prize, at least half the team members must identify as women.
    3. To win the Best Algorithm design prize, the team must submit a written explanation of their algorithm.
